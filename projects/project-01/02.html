Edon crush, 2023

SONY SLT-A58   35 mm   f/5   1/60

This photo holds a bizarre place in my heart.

///

This and many other I took on that night received so much praise from my peers that I began to start publicly sharing my work once more – the first time since 2018.
At the same time, the social location and context of this shot – The Edon Club – leaves my current perception of this work to be a lot more complex. Are my photos an explicit endorsement of my clients, their aesthetics and their values?
