II. Tool for One -> Kojo Aziabor League Report
Inspired by the UIs of various soccer video games across the past decade, I wanted to make a newspaper website: a) showcasing the aggregate score of my matches in tennis, ping pong, FIFA, and more against my twin brother, b) allowing either of us to securely update the score at any time, and c) helping us immerse ourselves in the competition with press conferences after every result. Now, my brother finally has the established record of wins and losses he has always wanted. It states the obvious fact I am better but I'm glad he has it, I guess.

Access the documentation on the site’s description page.
III. Final Presentation -> Hyperlinks (w/ Zack)
Through a historical survey of hyperlinks and a crafted prototype on a new way to visually and dynamically search the web, Zach and I explore how hyperlinks can be seen past their conventional use and return to the original vision and purpose.
